Here’s What’s Included in Small Business Essentials:

GS1 Canada Subscription

A subscription to GS1 Canada is your gateway to the world of global standards and the many benefits available to help you on your product journey. Your GS1 Canada subscription provides access to flexible tools, resources and support, helping to ensure you’re providing trading partners with the content they need to list your products while managing your operational resources efficiently and effectively.


As a critical first step, your GS1 Canada subscription provides you with the GS1 Global Trade Item Numbers (GTINs) you need for your products and barcodes, to help meet requirements of major Canadian retailers and etailers like Amazon for all products being sold to carry GS1 GTINs.


Every GS1 Canada subscription includes the following tools, services and resources:

  • Annual GTIN License
  • myGS1
    • Your personalized online hub for integrated, secure and quick access to your GS1 Canada tools and services in one place, including:
    • Visibility to your account status and history at a glance with easy-to-find invoices and online payment options
    • Centralized account management for changing passwords and personal account contact information
    • Subscription and company profile management: Company administrators can quickly add new users, manage existing users and update company contact information directly within myGS1
    • Visibility to data and image content major Canadian trading partners require to enable their business processes
  • Educational Resources
    • GS1 Canada’s Learning Zone gives you 24/7 online access to user-friendly bilingual training videos and supporting resources.
  • GTIN Ombudsman
    • The GTIN Ombudsman is a distinct role that serves as a single source of truth for any GTIN-related questions or concerns, including GTIN allocation rules, to ensure the correct assignment of GTINs to products.

Barcode Generator

Your all-in-one tool to efficiently create high-resolution barcode symbols for your products.

Data Loading and Sharing

Easily load your product data and quickly share it with retailers, distributors and other data recipients with confidence, knowing that you’re providing the data they need to list new products and that meets Canadian regulatory requirements.

TrueSource™ Dashboard

Once you have your product data loaded, this data excellence tool provides visibility to product gaps and missing information through easy-to-read graphs and charts to ensure your trading partners have access to the product details they need.

Recall

A standardized notification tool that ensures you're ready to respond quickly if your products need to be removed from the market due to a product recall.

Personalized Consulting

Get the help you need to get your products to market with one hour of personalized one-on- one consulting.
